Customizing Fonts

The font used to display items within the Feed Subscriptions and Headline section can be configured via the Fonts Dialog.

To bring up the Fonts Dialog, go to Tools | Options and click on the Fonts dialog. This should bring up the dialog seen in Figure 1.


Figure 1. Fonts Dialog.

There are five different font settings listed, Read, Unread, Flag, Referrer, and Error. To change any setting, just click on the setting and click the Change... button. You can see a preview of the font in the Sample box.

The Settings

Read (normal)
This is the font used for a normal feed item that has already been read.

Unread
Unread items are rendered with this font setting.

Flag
When you flag an item, a copy is put in one of the flagged items folders. Within the original feed, the flagged post is rendered with this font setting.

Referrer
If a post contains a link to your feed (as configured in the Manage Identities dialog), it is rendered with this font. This is a nice visual way to see who among the feeds you are subscribed to are linking to your own site.

In the example given by Figure 1, Flagged items are rendered in a bold red font.
