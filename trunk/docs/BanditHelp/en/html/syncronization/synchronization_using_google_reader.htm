Synchronization using Google Reader

Selecting Google Reader allows you to remotely synchronize the state of your feeds in RSS Bandit with the Google Reader service which is a web-based RSS reader. Users can synchronize the state of their subscribed feeds (read/unread posts, new/deleted feeds, etc) between RSS Bandit and their account on Google Reader. This allows the best of both worlds where one can use both a rich desktop client (RSS Bandit) and a web-based RSS reader (Google Reader) without having to worry about marking things as read in both places.
